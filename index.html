about our site

This site presents observations of the weather, its forecasts for all cities of the world. In this version, weather icons are shown next to textual forecasts for easier visual reference.

In the "Forecast" section, it is possible to search for a weather forecast for a specific city, weather data at a given time will be displayed, this information will be the most extended, as well as weather data for 5 days, which indicates the minimum and maximum temperatures on that day.

You have the opportunity to add the cities you like and the necessary cities to your favorites, where in the section with the same name you can view the weather for all saved cities at once.

The media are allowed to make the information presented on this website available to the public. In this case, reference should be made to this source.

Site navigation is represented by a "cobweb", that is, you can go to any other section, being in the selected one, as well as from the "Favorites" section, you will go to the "footer" of the site, which contains minimum information.
